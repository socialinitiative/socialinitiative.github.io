What is our initiative all about?

“E for Educate”, An e-waste drive with a charitable twist is being undertaken by CEG Tech Forum – the technical hub of College of Engineering, Guindy, Anna University in association with Kurukshetra 2016 under the patronage of UNESCO. This drive aims to spread awareness about the careless disposal and dumping of e-waste.

With the growing technological advancements, we tend to change smartphones every year and switching from CRT to LCD to LED to 3D to 4K (Phew!) has led to unprecedented accumulation of e-waste. Not all of us go ahead to sell our old gadgets once we get hang of new ones and overtime, our stuffs become unusable when you discover them in your loft and finally clear them on the day of yearly cleaning-your-house regime. When you dump them in the garbage bin at the end of your street, it gets dumped straight in a landfill which leads to numerous environmental hazards.

It is time to act. It is our responsibility to recycle and dispose our e-waste properly and pave way for a sustainable environment.

e-Waste

So what are classified under e-waste? Electronic waste comprises anything from discarded computers, workplace equipment, entertainment devices, mobile phones, television sets, home appliances and more. One has to know that it includes used electronics that are destined to reuse, recycle, resale. How to Join Us

    It's fantastic that you care about the world that we live in! Are you ready to start saving Earth? :) Don't worry, participation is as simple as it can get:

  • Step 1:

    Make sure your school has taken part in our e-waste drive. If not, be our messenger to spread the good word.

  • Step 2:

    Once your school is aboard, we will place collection bins at a prominent location in your school. With that in place, all you have to do is pack a bag or two of your e-waste along with the school bag, go to school as usual, and drop it of in the collection bins.

  • Step 3:

    If you have any doubts, ask for your staff coordinator from your school for our e-waste drive and get it clarified.

  • Step 4:

    Once you have disposed e-waste from your home, be our messenger and spread awareness in your neighborhood, and do as much as you can for the society.

  • Step Ultimate:

    If everything is done, and you feel your school has done enough to sustain the environment, sit back and wait for the official commemoration from our side to honor your school and compliment each of the contributors with loads of goodies, badges and more.
